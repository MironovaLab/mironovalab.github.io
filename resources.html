CoolHormones

Dataset

An integrated and well-aligned transcriptomic resource capturing stable, time-resolved patterns of gene expression during chilling stress in Arabidopsis. The resource is the result of a comprehensive data survey of the chilling stress-induced transcriptomes in the model species Arabidopsis thaliana L. ecotype Col-0.
